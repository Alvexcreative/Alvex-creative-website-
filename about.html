About Alvex Creative

We help local businesses compete — and win — online

Alvex Creative was founded on a simple belief: local businesses deserve a strong online presence. We help businesses across Leicestershire compete with larger brands through professional websites that are built to be seen and designed to deliver results.

Our story

Built for the businesses that keep Leicestershire's high streets running

Tradespeople, salons, barbers, independent shops and local service providers are the backbone of our community — but too often they're invisible online, losing work to competitors with deeper pockets and slicker websites.

We started Alvex Creative to change that. We combine modern, professional design with strong SEO foundations and proven lead-generation systems, so local businesses can be found on Google, look the part, and turn more visitors into paying customers. No inflated agency price tags — just websites that genuinely work for the people who run them.

Our mission

To help local businesses grow through websites that earn their keep

Every decision we make comes back to one question: will this help our client get more customers? If the answer is no, we don't do it.
